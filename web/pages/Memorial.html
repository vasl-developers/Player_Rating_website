Memorial List

This list contains the names of ASLers who are no longer with us, having passed on. We wish to pay tribute to them as friends and for their contribution to our game.

The tournament games and overall records of these individuals are included in the database and the various results displays on this site as a mark of respect and to ensure that their participation in our hobby is never forgotten.

To each of them: You are safely home, rest in peace and roll low.
